AI Testcase Generator

Use the chatbot widget to generate test cases from user stories, then copy or clear output with one click.

Banking / Sports / Construction / Real Estate

How to use

Step 1: Paste your own user story into the text box, or choose a sample story from the buttons below.

Step 2: Click Generate Testcases to create the AI testcase prompt instantly.

Step 3: Click the Open Langflow Chat button to open the Langflow chat widget.

Step 4: Ask the Langflow chat to generate detailed test cases for the story you entered.

Step 5: Use Copy Output to copy the generated text, or Clear Chat to reset the story and output box.

Tip: Sample stories cover banking, sports, construction, and real estate domains so you can test the generator quickly.
